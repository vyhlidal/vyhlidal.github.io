How to Use the Business Calculator

Hi there! As your business coach, I'm here to guide you through using this handy Profit Potential Calculator. Just follow these simple steps to analyze your business's performance for a specific period, like a month or a year:

  1. Fill in your numbers: In the table, you'll find several metrics related to your business. Enter your current values for each metric under the "Current" column.
  2. Set your improvement goals: In the "Improvement (in %)" column, adjust the percentage of improvement you'd like to achieve for each metric. Think about the growth you want to see!
  3. Calculate your potential: Click the "Calculate" button to see how the improvements you set would impact your business. The "Improved" column will display the updated values for each metric based on your improvement goals.
  4. Visualize your success: Below the input table, you'll find a chart that shows the difference in net profit before and after the improvements. This visualization will help you understand the potential impact of your growth strategies on your business's bottom line.

Remember, this calculator is a tool to help you explore different growth scenarios. As you fine-tune your strategies, feel free to adjust the improvement percentages and see how it affects your results. Good luck, and here's to your business success!
