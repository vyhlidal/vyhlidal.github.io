How to Use this Calculator
  1. Enter current values: In the table, input your current values for each metric under the "Current" column.
  2. Set improvement goals: Adjust the percentage of improvement for each metric in the "Improvement (in %)" column.
  3. Calculate results: Click "Calculate" to see the updated values based on your improvement goals in the "Improved" column.
  4. Visualize impact: The chart below the table shows the difference in net profit before and after the improvements.

Use this calculator to explore growth scenarios and adjust the improvement percentages as needed. Good luck with your business growth!
